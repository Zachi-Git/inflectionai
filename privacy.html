Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2025  ·  Last updated: July 13, 2026

InflectionAI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

DataWhen collectedPurpose
Email addressWhen you subscribe or check subscription statusSubscription management, account activation
Payment dataAt checkoutProcessed by PayPal or Stripe — we never see your card details
Transaction IDsAfter payment completionFraud prevention, dispute resolution
Usage preferencesLocally in your browser (localStorage)Remembering your symbol, timeframe, theme settings
Server logsOn each API requestDebugging, performance monitoring

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any financial account details. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify and activate your subscription
  • Send subscription confirmation and expiry notices (via email)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain platform security and prevent abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Payment Processors

Payments are handled by:

  • Stripe — card, Alipay, WeChat Pay. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal — PayPal balance, bank, or card. See PayPal's Privacy Policy.
  • Manual (USDT) — wallet address and TX hash submitted directly to us; no third-party processor.

These processors may have their own data collection and retention policies which we do not control.

4. Data Storage and Security

Subscription records (email, plan, expiry date) are stored in a SQLite database on our server hosted by Render. Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). User preferences are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers.

We retain subscription records for up to 2 years after expiry for tax and dispute purposes, then delete them.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@inflectionai.io. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The platform stores the following in your browser's localStorage:

  • Theme preference (light/dark)
  • Preferred language
  • Default symbol and timeframe
  • Watchlist symbols

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: privacy@inflectionai.io

General support: support@inflectionai.io
